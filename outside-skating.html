Prices

Hire a couple in the skate rental or get your own towed. We accept contact payment as well as payment with Dankort, Visa, MasterCard and Visa Elektron.

You can rent skates at the ice rink or hand in your own skates for grinding.

Skate rental


50 kr. couple.
Clip card with 6 clips: 250 kr.
1 clip applies to the rental of a pair of skates.

Remember identification when renting skates as it is used as a deposit.
Need your skates to be sanded? Then hand them in at the skate rental during business hours and have it arranged with us. Payment is payable on submission. It takes approx. 3 days.

Free entrance with AarhusCARD

Med et AarhusCARD kan du gratis leje skøjter i skøjteudlejningen ved skøjtebanen. Du får også gratis entré til museer og andre attraktioner. Du kører gratis med kollektiv trafik i Region Midtjylland samt lufthavnsbusser fra Aarhus og Billund. AarhusCard kan blandt andet købes i DOKK1.
